Memento Mori

"Memento Mori" is a Latin phrase meaning "Remember you must die." This concept has been depicted in art to remind viewers of the inevitability of death, encouraging them to reflect on the transient nature of life.

Amor Fati

"Amor Fati" translates to "Love of Fate." It embodies an attitude of embracing everything that happens in life, including suffering and loss, as necessary and beneficial.
